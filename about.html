About Al Doha

Nurturing global connections through premium Egyptian agricultural excellence since 2015.

Our Roots

Cultivating Excellence in Egyptian Agriculture

Al Doha for Import & Export specializes in sourcing and exporting the finest Egyptian herbs, spices, seeds, and legumes. Our mission is to provide the global market with natural, high-quality products while maintaining sustainable farming and processing practices that respect the fertile land of Egypt.

We believe that the path to global leadership in agricultural export is paved with integrity, quality, and a deep respect for our heritage. From the fertile banks of the Nile to your doorstep, we ensure every product carries the essence of Egyptian quality.
