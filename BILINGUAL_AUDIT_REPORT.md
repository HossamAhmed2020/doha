# BILINGUAL CONTENT AUDIT & OPTIMIZATION REPORT
## Project: Al Doha For Import & Export Website
## Date: June 16, 2026

### 1. Executive Summary
The Al Doha website has been transformed from a generic "Import & Export" platform into a professional, authoritative, and SEO-optimized showcase for a premium Egyptian agricultural export company. The content now specifically targets international B2B buyers interested in medicinal herbs, aromatic plants, spices, seeds, and grains.

### 2. Strategic Tone & Branding
- **Tone:** Shifted from generic trade language to "Premium Agricultural Specialist".
- **Keywords Integrated:** Egyptian Herbs Exporter, Medicinal Herbs Supplier, Spices Export Company, Bulk Agricultural Products, Grains Supplier, Nile Delta Harvest.
- **Brand Voice:** Professional, trustworthy, international, and export-focused.

### 3. Page-by-Page Improvements

| Page | Improvements Made |
